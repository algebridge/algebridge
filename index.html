CHAPTER 01

Algebridge is a student-led initiative that helps students overcome struggles in Algebra

Algebridge was founded in 2026 by Ivan Dubovyi, who saw firsthand how many peers fall behind when Algebra 1 begins - and felt driven to change that

We focus on Algebra 1 because it's the critical turning point where students either build a strong foundation or begin to lose confidence. Our mission is to make sure every student makes it across that bridge

CHAPTER 02

Our mission: ensure every kid truly understands Algebra

Algebra 1 is more than just a subject - it's the foundation for everything that comes next. Fractions, equations, functions, and variables form the language of advanced math, science, and technology

Our mission is to make sure no student falls behind at this critical stage - because understanding Algebra 1 means understanding the beginning of it all

CHAPTER 03

Why we do this — and why it matters

We've Seen the Struggle

Our founder saw how quickly students begin to lose confidence when Algebra 1 starts. That moment sparked a decision to act — and Algebridge was built from that experience

Algebra 1 Is the Foundation

Every advanced math course, from geometry to calculus, builds on Algebra 1. Getting it right here isn't optional. It's the foundation for everything that follows

Every Kid Deserves to Succeed

No student should fall behind simply because they didn't have the right support at the right time. We're here to make sure they don't
